fish ugali

ingridients

  • fresh fish
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • vegetable oil
  • garlic
  • lemon

instructions

  1. pat the fish dry with a paper towel
  2. season with salt and pepper
  3. heat oil or butter in a skillet over medium-high heat
  4. cook skin side down for 3-4 mins and then flip and cook for 2 more mins
  5. fish is done when it flakes easily with a fork
  6. serve with hot ugali cooked with soko ugali
