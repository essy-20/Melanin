rice peas

white hot rice and well cooked peas

ingridients

  • 1 cup of biryani rice
  • 1 cup of peas
  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • tomatoe paste
  • half a cup of cooking oil
  • salt

instructions

  1. wash rice and place in boiling water with salt and oil
  2. simmer the rice
  3. let the rice rest
  4. fluff and serve with peas stew
